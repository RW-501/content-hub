Step-by-Step: How to Cook Pizza Hut Copycat Pan Pizza

  3. First Rise (1 hour): Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it double in size.

  4. Prepare Pans (5 min): Generously oil your cast-iron skillet or cake pans‚Äîlike, pour 3 tbsp oil in each. You want shallow puddles.
