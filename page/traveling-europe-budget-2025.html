Cultural Insights and Travel Tips

  • Language: While English is widely spoken, learning a few phrases in the local language can enhance your experience and is appreciated by locals.

  • Local Etiquette: In many European countries, it's customary to greet with a handshake or a kiss on both cheeks. Always be polite and respectful.

  • Safety: Europe is generally safe for travelers, but always be aware of your surroundings, especially in crowded tourist areas.

Packing Essentials for European Travel

  • Comfortable Shoes: You'll be walking a lot, so pack comfortable footwear.

  • Power Adapter: Europe uses different plug types, so bring a universal adapter.

  • Travel Insurance: Always have travel insurance to cover unexpected events.
