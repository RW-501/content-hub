Ingredients

For the Pumpkin Cream:

  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (or coconut cream for a dairy-free option)

  • 2 tbsp pumpkin puree

  • 1-2 tbsp sugar or maple syrup (adjust to taste)

  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

  • Pinch of cinnamon

For the Cold Brew:

  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee or 1 cup cold brew concentrate

  • Ice cubes

  • Optional: splash of milk or oat milk

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Make the Pumpkin Cream

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together heavy cream (or alternative), pumpkin puree, sugar, vanilla extract, and cinnamon until slightly thickened.

  2. Use a hand frother or whisk vigorously to create a light, airy texture. The goal is a creamy, pourable topping that floats beautifully on your coffee.

Step 2: Prepare the Cold Brew

  1. Fill a glass with ice cubes.

  2. Pour your cold brew or chilled coffee over the ice.

  3. Add a splash of milk or milk alternative if desired.

Step 3: Assemble the Drink

  1. Slowly pour the pumpkin cream over the coffee so it floats on top.

  2. Optional: sprinkle a pinch of cinnamon or pumpkin spice on top.

Step 4: Serve & Enjoy
Sip through the creamy pumpkin layer first or stir it into the coffee for an all-in-one fall explosion of flavor.

Quick How-To Tips

  • Froth it right: For extra creaminess, chill your cream before whisking.

  • Make ahead: Pumpkin cream can be made up to 2 days in advance‚Äîjust store in the fridge.

  • Adjust sweetness: Taste as you go. Some coffee is naturally bitter, so start with less sugar.

  • Layering trick: Pour slowly over the back of a spoon to float the pumpkin cream perfectly.
