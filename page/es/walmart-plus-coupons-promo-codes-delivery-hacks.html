Coupons, Promo Codes, and How They Work

Walmart+ isn’t magic, but it makes coupons and promo codes easier to use:

  • Digital Coupons: Found in the Walmart app, these apply automatically at checkout—no clipping required.

  • Stackable Savings: Combine Walmart+ benefits with manufacturer coupons or sale prices for maximum savings.

  • Promo Codes: Occasionally, Walmart+ will offer codes for free trials, discounts on subscriptions, or seasonal perks.

Pro Tip: Keep a running list in your phone or a browser extension like Honey or Rakuten to make sure you never miss a stacking opportunity.

Delivery Options That Make Life Easy

Walmart+ delivery is versatile:

  • Same-Day Delivery: Great for essentials you forgot until now.

  • Next-Day Delivery: Ideal for bulk items or recurring purchases.

  • Scheduled Delivery: Pick a window that matches your schedule.

  • In-Store Pickup: Add items online and grab them at your local store without leaving your car.

Tips to Save on Delivery Fees

  1. Order in Bulk: Large orders maximize free delivery benefits.

  2. Combine Items: Add miscellaneous items to hit free delivery thresholds.

  3. Check for Deals: Some items have “free delivery” even without Walmart+.

Walmart+ Hacks and Tips

FAQ About Walmart+

Q1: How much does Walmart+ cost?
$12.95/month or $98/year. Many users find the savings on delivery and fuel cover the cost.
Q2: Can I use Walmart+ with regular Walmart coupons?
Yes! Walmart+ benefits stack with manufacturer coupons and app-exclusive deals.
Q3: Is delivery available everywhere?
Delivery depends on location and item availability. Same-day delivery is more common in urban areas.
Q4: Does Walmart+ include groceries?
Absolutely. Fresh produce, meat, pantry staples, and household essentials are all covered.
Q5: Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel online or via the app without penalties.
