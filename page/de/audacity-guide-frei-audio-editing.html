üí° Tips & Tricks to Master Audacity

  1. Noise Reduction: Use the "Noise Reduction" effect to eliminate background hums and hisses.

  2. Keyboard Shortcuts: Learn shortcuts like Ctrl+Z for undo and Ctrl+Shift+Z for redo to speed up your workflow.

  3. Use Labels: Insert labels to mark important points in your audio, aiding in navigation and editing.

  4. Normalize Audio: Ensure consistent volume levels across your tracks with the "Normalize" effect.

  5. Save Projects Regularly: Avoid losing progress by saving your work frequently.
