Webull: The Trading Platform That Doesn't Hold Your Hand

In the world of retail trading, where flashy ads and promises of easy riches abound, Webull stands out as the platform that doesn't sugarcoat the reality. It's not here to hold your hand or tell you what to buy. Instead, it offers a sleek, no-frills interface packed with powerful tools for those who are ready to take control of their financial destiny.

The Rise of Webull

Founded in 2016 by Wang Anquan, Webull quickly gained traction among traders seeking a more sophisticated alternative to platforms like Robinhood. With its commission-free trading model and a suite of advanced features, Webull attracted a user base that appreciated its transparency and depth.

In April 2025, Webull made headlines by going public through a SPAC merger with SK Growth Opportunities, debuting on the Nasdaq under the ticker symbol "BULL." The move was a testament to the platform's growing influence in the trading world.
