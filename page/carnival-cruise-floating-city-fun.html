Q2: Are Carnival Cruises family-friendly?
A2: Absolutely. There are kids’ clubs, teen lounges, water parks, and family excursions—but also plenty of adults-only areas if you want peace.
Q3: What’s the best time to cruise?
A3: Depends on your itinerary. Caribbean trips peak from December to April; summer offers longer daylight and fewer crowds in some regions.
Q4: Is Carnival safe during COVID times?
A4: Carnival follows strict health protocols, including vaccination requirements, enhanced cleaning, and onboard medical staff. Always check the latest policies before booking.
Q5: Can I work remotely on a Carnival Cruise?
A5: Yes, but Wi-Fi is available at a cost and may vary by ship. Consider it a luxury perk rather than a full remote office.
