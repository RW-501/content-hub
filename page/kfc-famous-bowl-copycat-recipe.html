KFC Famous Bowl Copycat Recipe – Comfort Food in a Bowl

Imagine a bowl layered with crispy chicken, creamy mashed potatoes, corn, cheese, and savory gravy—a bite of nostalgia that hits every comfort-food craving at once. That’s the KFC Famous Bowl, and today, we’re bringing it into your kitchen with our contenthub.guru-approved copycat recipe.

Whether you’re hosting a family night, craving a late-night indulgence, or just need a reason to celebrate Thursday, this recipe will satisfy your fried-chicken-loving soul.

Ingredients – Original & Alternative Options

Chicken Layer

  • 2 cups cooked fried chicken (shredded) – or grilled chicken strips for a healthier twist

  • Optional: 1 teaspoon smoked paprika for extra flavor

Mashed Potato Layer

  • 2 cups mashed potatoes (homemade or instant)

  • 2 tablespoons butter

  • ¼ cup milk (or cream for extra richness)

  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Corn Layer

  • 1 cup sweet corn (canned, frozen, or fresh kernels)

  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, if corn is bland)

Gravy Layer

  • 1 cup brown gravy (homemade or store-bought)

  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce (optional, for depth)

Cheese Layer

  • ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese – or Colby Jack or mozzarella for a different flavor twist

Optional Toppings

  • Crispy fried onions

  • Fresh chopped parsley for garnish

  • Hot sauce for spice

Alternative Ingredients Tips:

  • For mashed potatoes: swap for cauliflower mash for a low-carb option.

  • For chicken: swap for vegan fried chicken for a plant-based Famous Bowl.

  • For gravy: try mushroom gravy for a richer, earthy taste.

Step-by-Step Instructions – Build Your Famous Bowl

Step 1: Prepare the Mashed Potatoes

  1. Heat your mashed potatoes in a pot or microwave until warm.

  2. Stir in butter, milk, salt, and pepper until smooth and creamy.

  3. Optional: whisk briefly for a fluffier texture.

Step 2: Cook or Heat Your Chicken

  1. If using leftover fried chicken, shred it into bite-size pieces.

  2. For grilled or baked chicken: season with salt, pepper, and smoked paprika, cook until golden, then shred.

Step 3: Warm the Corn

  1. Heat corn in a pan over medium heat with a little butter or in the microwave.

  2. Add sugar if desired and stir gently.

Step 4: Heat the Gravy

  1. Warm your gravy in a small saucepan.

  2. Add Worcestershire sauce if you want an extra umami punch.

Step 5: Layer Your Bowl

  1. Start with a generous layer of mashed potatoes at the bottom.

  2. Add a layer of corn.

  3. Place shredded chicken over the corn.

  4. Pour gravy over the chicken layer—don’t be shy; let it soak in.

  5. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.

  6. Optional: garnish with crispy fried onions or parsley.

Step 6: Serve and Enjoy

  • Serve immediately while hot.

  • Optional: drizzle with hot sauce for a zesty finish.

How to Make Your Famous Bowl Even Better

  • Extra Crunch: Toast breadcrumbs or crushed potato chips on top before serving.

  • Cheesy Variation: Mix cheese into the mashed potatoes layer for a gooey surprise.

  • Meal Prep Friendly: Assemble in a container and refrigerate for up to 3 days; heat in the oven for best results.

  • Kid-Friendly Version: Keep hot sauce and spices optional, layer smaller portions for picky eaters.

FAQ – KFC Famous Bowl Copycat Tips

Q1: Can I use store-bought mashed potatoes?

Absolutely! Instant mashed potatoes work just fine. Add a little extra butter or milk for creaminess.
Q2: Can I make this vegetarian or vegan?

Yes! Use plant-based fried chicken, dairy-free cheese, and mushroom gravy for a fully vegan bowl.
Q3: Can I bake this instead of assembling in layers?

Sure! Layer everything in a baking dish, top with cheese, and bake at 350°F (175°C) for 15–20 minutes until bubbly.
Q4: How do I keep the chicken crispy?

Shred fried chicken just before assembling, and avoid letting it sit in gravy too long. Assemble right before serving.
Q5: Can I freeze this for later?

Yes, but skip the cheese topping until reheating. Freeze in airtight containers and reheat in the oven or microwave.
