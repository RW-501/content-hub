Step 4: Monetization

By month two, traffic was picking up. Here’s how Maya turned visitors into dollars:

  1. Affiliate Marketing: She promoted products like travel guides, packing gear, and cooking utensils with affiliate links.

  2. Sponsored Posts: Small travel brands started reaching out to collaborate after seeing her engagement.

  3. Digital Products: Maya sold her first eBook: “50 Culinary Adventures You Must Try Before You Die.”

By month three, her blog earned $5,000—and the momentum was only just beginning.
