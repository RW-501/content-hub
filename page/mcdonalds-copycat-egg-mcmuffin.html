FAQ

Yes! Prepare the egg and bacon, store in an airtight container, then assemble and heat in the morning.
Q2: Can I freeze it?

Absolutely. Wrap tightly in foil and freeze for up to 1 month. Reheat in the oven or microwave.
Q3: How do I make it healthier?

Use whole-wheat muffins, egg whites, turkey bacon, and low-fat cheese.
Q4: Can I make it vegan?
