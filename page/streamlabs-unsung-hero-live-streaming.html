FAQ

Q1: Is Streamlabs free to use?

Yes, Streamlabs offers a free version with essential features. However, premium features are available through the Streamlabs Ultra subscription.

Q2: Can I stream on multiple platforms simultaneously?

Absolutely. Streamlabs supports multistreaming, allowing you to broadcast on various platforms at once.

Q3: How do I add custom alerts to my stream?
